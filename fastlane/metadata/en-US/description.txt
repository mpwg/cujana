Cujana is a calm allergy journal for people who want to understand symptoms and possible triggers in everyday life. The app helps you record symptoms quickly and connect them with relevant context such as pollen exposure, allergy risk, and weather conditions.

A new entry is intentionally simple: choose one or more symptoms, record severity, confirm the time, and add medication, tags, or notes when needed.

Cujana is designed to stay clear and private. The app is built for local use without an account and without a custom backend. Health-related observations stay on your device.

Key features:

- fast journal entries for one or more allergy symptoms
- severity, time, medication, tags, and notes
- overview of entries and current exposure
- pollen and allergy-risk context for orientation
- weather context through Apple Weather
- local processing without an account
- iOS interface with VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Dark Mode, high contrast, and Reduce Motion support

Cujana is not a diagnostic app and does not replace medical advice. It supports you in documenting observations for yourself or for conversations with doctors or allergy specialists.
